## Posting times (Pakistan audience — readers)

Based on when Pakistani reading/book audiences are most active:

| Slot | Time (PKT) | Why |
|------|-----------|-----|
| **Prime evening** | 8:30–10:30 PM | Highest engagement — people relax, scroll, read after dinner |
| **Afternoon** | 1:30–3:00 PM | Lunch break, students, homemakers |
| **Late night** | 11:00 PM–12:00 AM | Readers/night owls — high save + DM intent |
| **Morning** | 8:00–9:30 AM | Light — best for Stories, not main posts |

**Rule:** Post Reels & carousels at **9:00 PM** (prime). Use afternoon/late-night as backup. Stories all day.

---

## Format mix (30 days)

| Format | Count | Purpose |
|--------|-------|---------|
| Reels | 13 | Reach + discovery (algorithm favors these) |
| Carousels | 10 | Saves + deep sell (swipe = time spent = ranking) |
| Single posts | 5 | Aesthetic breathers + quick sell |
| Stories | Daily | Conversion engine (see `story-daily-rhythm.md`) |

Weekly rhythm: **3 Reels + 2–3 Carousels + 1 Single post + daily Stories.**

# Sales optimization notes

- **Every post ends with a clear order path** — DM keyword or link in bio. Never a dead end.
- **Carousels = highest save + sell.** They rank well and let people browse your catalog. 10 of 30 posts are carousels on purpose.
- **Reels = reach.** They bring new people; the carousels and Stories convert them.
- **Bundles (Day 15, 16)** raise average order value — push these when a single sale works.
- **Scarcity only when true** (Days 12, 18, 23). Never fake it — it breaks KHANUM's trust.
- **Stories every day** carry the daily selling load — see `story-daily-rhythm.md`. Reels/carousels build desire; Stories close.
- **Pin** Days 1, 3, and your best-performing reader-match Reel.
- **Repurpose:** every carousel slide can become a standalone Story with a link sticker.

# What to track (tie to `tracking/weekly-metrics.md`)

- Which DM keyword gets used most → make more of that book's content
- Carousel saves vs Reel reach → double down on the format that drives DMs
- Best posting time for YOUR audience after 2 weeks → shift the 9 PM slot if your Insights show otherwise
