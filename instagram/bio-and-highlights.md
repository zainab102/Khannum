## First-Time Setup Steps

1. Update bio text (copy from Option A above)
2. Set link to WhatsApp with pre-filled message
3. Create 4 highlight covers in Canva
4. Post 3–5 Stories into each highlight (use content from `highlights/` folder)
5. Film and post Week 1 Reels, then pin top 3
6. Enable "Allow sharing to stories" and reply to all DMs within 2 hours
